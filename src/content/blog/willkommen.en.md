# Welcome to the Erstwähler Forum 2026!

**Published on February 15, 2026**

We are excited to present the official website of the Erstwähler Forum 2026! Here you will find all information about the event on June 2, 2026 at the Stadeum in Stade.

## What awaits you?

The Erstwähler Forum is a unique cross-school event where first-time voters have the opportunity to experience local politics up close. Whether it's panel discussions, direct conversations with candidates, or the party market — there's a lot to discover!
